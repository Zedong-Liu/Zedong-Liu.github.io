About

I am a senior undergraduate student at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I will soon begin my Ph.D. studies at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS), advised by Prof. Dingwen Tao and Prof. Guangming Tan.

My research interests primarily lie in Machine Learning Systems and Data Compression, with a particular focus on efficient large-scale distributed inference and compression for massive scientific data. Recently, I have also become interested in the co-optimization of data compression techniques and large model inference systems.

Please feel free to contact me if you’re interested in relevant research topics or would like to discuss potential collaborations!
